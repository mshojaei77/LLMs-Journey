- **Concepts Covered**: `word embeddings`, `Word2Vec`, `GloVe`, `contextual embeddings`
- **Learning Resources**:
  - [![Illustrated Word2Vec](https://badgen.net/badge/Blog/Illustrated%20Word2Vec/cyan)](https://jalammar.github.io/illustrated-word2vec/)
